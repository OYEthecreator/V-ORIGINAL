THE PULSE: Tailor-Born / Vol.1

A moment. A shift. A new way to wear the now.

Tailor-Born is the untamed elegance of Africa — stitched in reverence, worn in rebellion. It is VOUX’s first brand drop — a concept born from the streets of Lagos, made by the raw essence of African tailoring and the precision of modern fashion. It draws power from ancestral stitchwork, earthy tones, and the rhythm of tradition reimagined. It is legacy — reshaped. Power in restraint. Luxury in storytelling. This is where heritage meets rebellion. Where timeless craftsmanship becomes the uniform of a new tribe. Tailor-Born doesn’t imitate Africa — it is Africa: layered, vibrant, bold without apology. Every piece is a conversation — between the past and now, between the sacred and the street.

Premiering Summer ‘26 — Lagos.
Crafted with history. Worn with destiny.
